Five members of the Microbial Lab from [*Atmosphere and Ocean Research Institute, The University of Tokyo*](https://www.aori.u-tokyo.ac.jp/index.html) are now participating " [The 14th ASME](https://sites.google.com/view/asme2024-taipei/home) " at *Taipei* from **18 Sep to 20 Sep**. 
Please be welcome to our poster sessions. Check the PDF details by clicking the names.

---
+ **[[^1] Dr. Chunqi Jiang (PD):]()** MP27 - "Ecological Characterization of Marine Chloroflexi through Metagenomic Big Data".

+ **[[^2] Hongwei Qin (D3):]()** MP24 - "Prokaryotes show different niche preferences between suspended and sinking particles in the western Pacific Ocean from tropical to subarctic regions"
  
+ **[[^1] Mako Takada (D2):]()** "Adaptation of Microbial Communities to Redox Environmental Changes in Subseafloor Sediments"

+ **[[^1] Yanfei Zhang (D2):]()** MP17 - "Bacterial Community Structure and Diversity of Tide Pools in the Subtropical Region on Amami Island, Japan"

+ **[[^1] Yuanyu Wang (M2):]()** "Genetic Diversity and Emission Spectrum Modulation in Marine Luminous Bacteria"

[^1]: [Yoshizawa Lab, AORI, UTokyo](https://genedynamics.aori.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/)

[^2]: [Microbial Oceanography Lab, AORI, UTokyo](http://ecosystem.aori.u-tokyo.ac.jp/microbiology-wp/)
